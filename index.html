Ultimate Meme Generator

Before we begin....

You can add middle line or remove a line from the meme

The up-down arrow toggles between lines

The trashcan icon clears the canvas entirely

Editing is done seperately per line

By default, the top line is selected

You can change the color, bold status, font size, font type and text alignment of a line

Changing the text of a line is done by the input field

To move a line, use the arrow / WASD keys on the keyboard, or just drag it via mouse or touch

Other options

Clicking on "I'm Flexible" gives you a random image from the gallery

The Download options saves the meme in a PNG format

You may use an image of your own via the Upload option

You can share your meme on Facebook!

Have fun!
